Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 8 ounce bag shredded cheese

Steps

  1. On the first layer place 3 noodles
  2. Next pour a thin layer of tomato sauce
  3. Then sprinkle on a layer of ground beef
  4. Finally sprinkle on shredded cheese
  5. Repeat these steps for three layers
  6. Put in the oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees
