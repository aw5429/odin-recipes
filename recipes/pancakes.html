Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100g Plain flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Caster sugar (optional)
  • Two Large Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Sunflower or vegetable oil, or melted butter if you donâ€™t want to use oil
  • 300ml Milk

Steps

  1. Combine the plain flour, eggs, milk, sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt in a jug and whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside the batter for 30 mins to rest if you have time (you can start cooking straight away if needed)!
  3. Put a medium frying pan over a medium heat hob and wipe carefully with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook the pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden
  5. Serve with pancakes topped with your favourite sweet or savoury toppings!
